Hello my name is Ko Watanabe, a 2nd year Ph.D. student at University of Kaiserslautern.

My PhD advisors are Jun.-Prof. Shoya Ishimaru, the junior-professor at University of Kaiserslautern and Prof. Andreas Dengel, the scientific director of German Research Center of Artificial Intelligence(DFKI).

I also experienced working 2 years of software engineering in DeNA, Japan . Current interest is on transfer (nudge) in learning, or a knowledge management.
